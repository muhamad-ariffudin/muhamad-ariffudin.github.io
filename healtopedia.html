Overview
Healtopedia is a web application designed to help Malaysians with low health literacy manage their health more effectively. Healtopedia collaborates with top-tier Hospital to give great service and a comprehensive range of healthcare services targeted to your specific requirements. Our website allows you to easily find, compare, and book services. Healtopedia's mission is to provide the greatest healthcare services to its consumers while also fostering a healthy community.
what was the problem we were trying to solve?
Addresses the issue of low health literacy among Malaysians by creating a web application that helps individuals better manage their health and understand their medical conditions.
what were my responsibilities?
1. Worked directly with the client to define and align business goals and user goals. 2. Conducted initial research and synthesized research findings 3. Created both low- and high-fidelity designs, including user flows, wireframes, UI graphics, and UI specifications, to develop fully functional websites. 4. Participated in QA sessions with project managers and engineers to fine-tune the final user experience

key insights from research
Healtopedia offers a convenient and easy-to-use platform for individuals to find and access a variety of healthcare services and packages, including health screenings and consultations, through a single website.
PROBLEM 1 : Limited availability
Other platform's selection of healthcare services and packages may be limited based on geographical location, as it only partners with a select number of specialist hospitals.
PROBLEM 2 : Quality assurance
While Others platform notes that its partners are award-winning hospitals, users may still be concerned about the quality and safety of the healthcare services and packages listed on the platform.
PROBLEM 3 : Accessibility
Users who do not have access to reliable internet or who are not comfortable using online platforms may face barriers to accessing healthcare services.

reflection
What's essential for mvp?
We only had four months from kickoff to launch so we had to move as efficiently as possible. Even though there were times I was hung up on small details, I learned that not everything can be done and that prioritizing and sometimes making a compromise is necessary for the team to move on.
WORKING WITH A CLIENT AND CROSS-FUNCTIONAL PARTNERS
This project was one of my end-to-end design project collaborating with a client and cross-functional partners. Working with these stakeholders taught me how to explain my design choices in a language that they understand and also be able to understand their language. I learned that different stakeholders might have different goals and that it’s important to over-communicate my decisions so that everyone’s goals are aligned.
FUTURE WORK
Because of the limited time and resources I wasn’t able to test the final product as much as I wanted to. I participated in a series of internal QA sessions to ensure the quality of the product, but I would’ve liked to conduct more usability testing with actual users, receive feedback and iterate if there was more time.
